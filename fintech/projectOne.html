Tokenization of Private Equity

Objective

The project’s primary purpose is to create a platform that allows any investor to participate in private equity funds that are generally illiquid and offered to corporations and the ultra-wealthy. We will raise funds through a crowd sale and then trade in the secondary market with a lock-in period of 10 years. Once the money is raised, the fund will then seed startups across a variety of industries in order to mimic S&P 500 performance.

Tokenization is the process of issuing a blockchain token (also known as a security token) that digitally represents a real tradable asset. These security tokens are created through a security token offering (STO).

A security token offering (STO)/tokenized IPO is a type of public offering in which tokenized digital securities, known as security tokens, are sold in cryptocurrency exchanges. Tokens can be used to trade real financial assets such as equities and fixed income, and use a blockchain virtual ledger system to store and validate token transactions.

  • Private equity funds can now raise funds through the creation of their own initial coin/security token offering
  • After the funds are raised, a market for investing is created for initial investors to sell and buy their coins
  • The expected fund returns are generated using statistical techniques such as the Autoregressive Moving Average (ARMA) and Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average (ARIMA).

Problem

Our token will offer the potential for a more efficient and fair financial market by allowing investors access to financial investment through digital currency, increasing accessibility into a market that is traditionally only offered to a select few.

Solution

Some key solutions that tokenization provides for both investors and sellers:

Liquidity: is the process of issuing a blockchain token (also known as a security token) that digitally represents a real tradable asset. These security tokens are created through a security token offering (STO).

Rapid settlement and cost-effective transactions: Token transactions are completed with smart contracts, and certain parts of the exchange process are automated. This automation will reduce administrative costs and allow for immutable same-day settlement.

Transparency: Tokens will have the token-holder’s rights and legal responsibilities embedded directly onto the token, along with an immutable record of ownership.

Compliance enforcement: Programs legal obligations into digital assets to ensure only eligible parties participate.

24/7 market: A marketplace that can run 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

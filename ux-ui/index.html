Put your creativity to work solving real-world design problems

Throughout the UX/UI Boot Camp, you will build a portfolio made up of three design studio projects that showcase the fundamental skills learned in user experience design, user interface design, and front end development.

Mobile App Design Project

The first project will turn market research into insights and ideas through storyboarding, user journey mapping, and usability testing. You will leverage tools like Adobe XD or Figma and iOS and Material Design guidelines to build a clickable prototype.

Nonprofit Site Redesign Project

On the second project, teams will collaborate to redesign a nonprofit website. You will leverage UI design fundamentals and best practices in usability heuristics and information architecture to create a clickable prototype with InVision, Adobe XD, and Figma.

Build Your Cause Project

The capstone project will be an open design challenge where you choose a business or cause of interest and recreate the experience. A combination of UX, UI, and front-end coding skills will be leveraged to create a live website prototype that creatively solves human-centered needs.

Each project is supported by industry professional instructors, trained UX TAs and tutors, classmates who help provide creative feedback, and career services team which helps polish learners' portfolios for professional interviews.

Digital Marketing Boot Camp teaches real-world solutions

Our cutting-edge curriculum gives learners the skills and tools needed to create marketing strategies and run, measure, automate, and optimize campaigns and websites.

Explore student projects

Changing the World One Website at a Time

Assignment: NonProfit Website Redesign and Optimization Project

Learners will put their newly learned digital marketing skills to work for a good cause. Teams will conduct market research on a topic close to their hearts, and plan a content strategy to spread the word and drive engagement. Learners will then create a website, using both creative and technical prowess. To build on this momentum, they will then analyze the site’s performance, and optimize it to rank well in search results.

Rewarding the Customer

Assignment: Loyalty Program Project

In our age of digital disruption, businesses’ physical locations often don’t see enough traffic to sustain sales. Students will learn to get customers back by devising a loyalty program to encourage visitors to frequent and become advocates for the business. Teams will create a multi-channel digital marketing plan and landing page for their loyalty program that is sure to bring them more site traffic and ultimately, more foot traffic.
